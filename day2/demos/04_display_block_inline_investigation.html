140 x 60

140 x 60

Try it: inspect an inline swatch in DevTools and add height: 200px in the Styles pane. Nothing happens - inline boxes don't have a height you can set. Change display to inline-block and suddenly it obeys. This is the single most common "why won't my box resize?" puzzle.

Getting boxes to sit side by side properly - evenly spaced, centred, equal height - is a job for Flexbox. That's this afternoon.
