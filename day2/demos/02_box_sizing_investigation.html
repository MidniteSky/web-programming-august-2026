Why is my box too wide?

Both boxes below ask for width: 300px with the same padding and border. Only one of them is actually 300px.

box-sizing: content-box (the default) - I am secretly 350px wide
box-sizing: border-box - I am exactly 300px wide, as asked

The one-line habit you'll see at the top of almost every stylesheet:

* {
    box-sizing: border-box;
}

It makes width mean what you expect. From now on, this goes at the top of our project stylesheet.
