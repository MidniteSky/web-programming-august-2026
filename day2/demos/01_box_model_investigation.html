Everything is a box

content only - text shoved against the border (yesterday's feeling)
padding: 20px - room to breathe inside the border
margin: 30px - this box also shoves itself away from its neighbours
padding: 10px 40px and margin: 20px 0 - different values per side

Try it: open DevTools (F12), click any box, and scroll the Styles pane to the bottom. There's a live box-model diagram - hover over its margin / border / padding / content bands and the matching part lights up on the page.
